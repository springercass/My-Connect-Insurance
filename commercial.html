Commercial Insurance

Commercial Auto Insurance

We at connect Insurance Group want your business to be safe and continue thriving by preventing the loss of your hard-earned revenue due to an auto accident on the job. You want to ensure that your vehicles, products, and drivers are insured in the event of property or vehicle damage, bodily injury, legal costs, and more. Since Commercial Auto Insurance coverage varies, we work with you to customize a policy that can include: Medical Payments, Bodily Injury Liability, Personal Injury Protection (PIP) Coverage, GAP Coverage, Comprehensive Coverage, Collision Coverage, Uninsured and Underinsured Motorist Coverage.

Commercial General Liability

With every business comes major risks, and you don't want to lose your hard earned revenue or even lose your business because of an accident. As a business owner, you aren't just responsible for ensuring that operations are running smoothly, but also keeping your employees and property insured from injuries and damage. We at Connect Insurance Group help you design a policy that tailored to your business' needs. Commercial General Liability can reduce or even prevent major financial loss if your business is sued or held legally responsible in the event of bodily injury or damage and can cover most medical expenses, attorney fees, settlements, and reimbursements covered.

Commercial Property

Accidents can and will happen, and the last thing you want as a business owner is to be under the wrong policy when it does and potentially go out of commission. With the proper Property Insurance policy from Connect Insurance Group, you can ensure that your building and your business property is protected from accidents, damage or a major financial loss. Your Property Insurance policy can include add-ons such as Builder’s Risk insurance which protects any materials or equipment from damage or loss sustained during construction or remodeling, or Equipment Breakdown coverage, which covers items like Machinery, electronics, or pressurized equipment from breakdowns or loss.

Workers Compensation

We understand that the success of your business depends on your employees and their hard work, expertise, and commitment and they rely on quality benefits and coverage plans that your business provides to work with peace of mind, knowing they are properly protected. Having a Workers Compensation plan allows your business to cover an employee's expenses from injuries such as; car accidents, back injuries, equipment malfunctions, and in very unfortunate situations, even death. It also covers other financial obligations such as; medical bills, replacement of lost wages, legal fees in the case of a lawsuit, and funeral costs.

Landlord Insurance

As the owner of an apartment, home, condo, or other property, you’ll want to be prepared in the event that your investment is damaged. Landlord Insurance can cover damage to your property or other attached structures from a fire, natural disaster, damage caused by tenants, or medical payments in the event of injury and Legal counsel and fees in the event of a related lawsuit.

Business Owner's Package

A Business Owner's Package policy can cover an arry of a business' insurance needs. It often includes vehicle, property damage, business income, property, and bodily injury, as well as other types of basic insurance needs. This is a valuable option for business' that want easy comprehensive coverage and to save time and money.
