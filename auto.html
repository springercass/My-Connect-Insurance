Auto Insurance

Liability Coverage

If an accident occurs, you're going to want to have the right insurance to cover yourself personally, legally and financially. Liability Coverage, will protect you if you’re held responsible for an accident by having medical payments available for injuries sustained to you or the other driver, passengers, and pedestrians, as well as any bills for property damage or legal fees.


Auto liability coverage has two main parts: Bodily Injury and Property Damage Liability


Bodily Injury Liability helps pay for costs associated with the other person’s injuries, which includes medical expenses, pain and suffering and loss of revenue from time spent out of work.
Property Damage Liability handles expenses that come from damage to another driver’s vehicle or property. If the accident is very serious, Liability Coverage will reduce the risk of major loss from expenses that could result in the loss of your home, savings, and income.

Collision Coverage

For a lot of people, their car is a big investment and if you get into an accident, you want to make sure that you protect that investment. Whether the damage is a result of an accident with another vehicle or an object, you want that damage to be covered by our insurance. The right amount of Collision Coverage depends on factors like the age and year of your car, as well as your financial situation and we at Connect Insurance Group can help you find the best coverage that works for you and your unique situation. You may also want to add-on Diminishing Deductible, GAP Coverage, and Accident Forgiveness to your plan for better protection.


Diminishing Deductible rewards safe drivers with a discount and can even eliminate the deductible that they pay if they were to get into an accident.

GAP Coverage covers the difference when a settlement from an accident is less than the actual amount you owe on your vehicle loan. We will make sure that you get paid enough to cover what you still owe on your cars financing or leasing agreement.

Accident Forgiveness ensures that your rates won’t increase just because of a single accident.

Comprehensive Coverage

Life is full of surprises, and we realize that things can happen to your car both on and off the road. Your security and peace of mind don't have to end when your not behind the wheel because Comprehensive Coverage insures your vehicle against damage and expenses that can happen when you're away. Anything can and will happen, that's why you want to be prepared, whether it's damages or loss resulting from theft, larceny, vandalism, broken glass, fire, hail, water, a flood, hitting a deer, falling objects, your crazy ex, or any other non-collision related incident. We also offer coverage for Roadside Assistance which provides you with benefits such as lockout assistance, jump starts, and towing all bundled into one comprehensive package.
