Personal Umbrella Insurance

Personal Umbrella Insurance is often overlooked but could actually be the best line of defense in the case of a financial catastrophe. If you were to be involved in a major car accident or someone is injured on your property, the amount you could be sued for could exceed what your home and auto insurance policies cover. In such a situation, an Personal Umbrella Insurance policy will protect your home, car, wages and assets from being taken to pay for the expenses.

Health Insurance

Healthcare can be expensive, thats why its important to have Health insurance to cover your healthcare needs and expenses. Not only will Health Insurance help you be prepared for when you need medical attention but will also help cover costs for preventative care such as screenings and regular checkups. It's important to note that the law also requires individuals under the Affordable Care Act to have some form of qualified Health Care Coverage.

Dental Coverage

Having the right dental insurance plan can help reduce the cost of preventative, basic and major dental operations. Preventative procedures are usually covered completely by dental insurance while basic procedures such as fillings, simple extractions and some deep cleaning procedures are almost completely covered. As for major operations, that will depend on the policy you choose to go with and an insurance specialist at connect Insurance Group can help you find the right one for you.

Life Insurance

In the unfortunate event of a untimely death, you want to know your family and loved ones will be taken care of. Financial obligations associated with death can be tremendous and add on to the stress of losing a loved one, this is why it is important to consider a life insurance policy. The right policy will grant a trusted person with a lump sum of money to cover the costs of death as well as provide in place of missing income from someone who is deceased.
